2 Fast 2 Furious

Roman is introduced as a former demolition derby driver with a knack for fast-talking. After being released on house arrest, he teams up with Brian to infiltrate a drug lord's operation, earning a pardon in return. Despite their past, Roman and Brian rekindle their friendship during this mission.

About

Roman Pearce is the childhood friend of Brian O'Conner and a former convict who harbored stolen cars. He won his freedom after taking down drug lord Carter Verone in an undercover assignment by U.S. Customs with Brian in Miami. He then became a member of Dominic Toretto's crew when Dominic, Mia Toretto and Brian planned to steal money from corrupt Portuguese businessman, Hernan Reyes.[1] Roman also became partners with Tej Parker.

Biography

Roman Pearce was born on April 12, 1978 and raised in Barstow, California. As a child, he befriended another Barstow local, Brian O'Conner. Roman and Brian grew up together, and often caused trouble together and played sports.[2] Roman and Brian attended high school together, and often dated the same girls, Roman dating the girls Brian was no longer romantically involved with.[2][note 1] When Roman and Brian attended prom, Roman presumably had sex with an undesirable girl nicknamed "No Knees" Denise

Infiltrating Carter Verone's Empire

Following Brian's arrest in Miami by Special Agent Bilkins and U.S. Customs Agent Markham, Brian agreed to help Bilkins and Markham expose and arrest drug lord, Carter Verone, if he could choose who would go undercover with him. Bilkins and Brian traveled to Barstow where Roman was making a living a demolition derby driver. When Roman won the derby, he spotted Brian in the stands with Bilkins and headed for his RV.

Reconciling with Brian

Brian and Roman worked with Tej to lose Bilkins's and Markham's tracking devices on their Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution VII and Mitsubishi Eclipse Spyder. During that time, Roman finally took responsibility for the actions that led to his arrest and reconciled with Brian, who he longer blamed. Brian admitted that letting Dom go was largely motivated by the guilt he harbored for being unable to help Roman. With help from Tej, they orchestrated a "scramble", after driving into a garage they later had hundreds of race cars come out, having switched cars with Tej and Suki while in the garage.

Aftermath of Carter Verone

After the arrest of Verone, Roman and Brian's criminal records were cleared. They each took their share of Verone's money, unbeknownst to Bilkins, and made plans to open a garage in Miami together. It is unknown what happened to these plans as Brian later joined the FBI, leaving Miami to return to Los Angeles. It's likely that Roman either stayed in Miami, as he said he would, or returned to Barstow
